What's TEDx Divine Public School Youth 2027?

One Day. Bold Voices.
Infinite Ideas.

"Ideas worth spreading aren't just those that comfort us — they're the ones that disturb us just enough to make us grow."

On 9th January 2027, Divine Public School, Puthoor will open its doors to an extraordinary gathering of thinkers, innovators, educators, and young voices. TEDx Divine Public School Youth 2027 brings together speakers from education, entrepreneurship, innovation, leadership, social impact, science, and the arts — all under one roof.

Under the theme "The Narrative Shift: Redefining Possible," we explore how changing the stories we believe about ourselves, our institutions, and our future can unlock extraordinary potential. This is a platform where student voices stand alongside accomplished leaders — where curiosity meets courage.

9 January 2027 Puthoor, Kerala Divine Public School

What is TEDx?

TEDx is a global program of independently organized events licensed by TED, dedicated to discovering and sharing ideas that matter in local communities around the world.

Our Mission

To foster curiosity, inspire innovation, and cultivate meaningful conversations across generations — spreading ideas that challenge assumptions and inspire action.

Who It's For

Students, educators, parents, entrepreneurs, and anyone passionate about ideas. Join a community united by curiosity, creativity, and the courage to reimagine what's possible.

Created in the spirit of TED's mission of "Ideas Worth Spreading," TEDx events bring people together to experience live speakers and thought-provoking conversations that inspire curiosity, understanding, and action. In the spirit of TED's mission, TEDx events are designed to spark deep discussion and connection at the local level.

TEDx Divine Public School Youth is an independently organized TEDx event operated under license from TED. This event is organized by Divine Public School and is not organized by TED Conferences, LLC.

About the Theme

The Narrative Shift:
Redefining Possible

Every generation inherits a set of assumptions about what can and cannot be achieved. Yet progress has always belonged to those willing to challenge those assumptions. In a world shaped by rapid technological advancement, evolving social realities, and unprecedented global interconnectedness, the ability to rethink established narratives has become one of the most important skills of our time.

Question Assumptions

How transformative ideas emerge from questioning conventional wisdom and reimagining what is possible.

Shape the Future

The role of young people, new models for learning, and innovations that reshape communities and industries.
